Stir Fry Yum Yum

A tasty, nutrient packed meal sure to fill you up!

Today I'm going to be sharing one of my favorite recipes taught to me by my mother. It's a simple recipe at its core, and I'm sure the rest of you will enjoy it as well. I will be sharing a basic version of the dish, however I will include some suggestions for other ingredients and substitutions at the end

While the meal is best enjoyed warm, it can be stored in the fridge for a number of days to enjoy for some quick but hardy leftovers. Additionally, sitting in the fridge will give it the time to congeal further and become nice and thick!
